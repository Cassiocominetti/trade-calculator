# 📊 Trade Calculator ++ Viver de Cripto (Live on Crypto) ++

English | [Portuguese Version](README.md)

Web application designed to calculate trading operations across multiple asset classes (crypto, stocks, commodities, forex), with a focus on risk management and position sizing.

> **Live application:** **[Calculadora de Trade ++ Viver de Cripto ++](https://calculadoraviverdecripto.vercel.app/)**

---

## 🧠 About the project

This application helps traders calculate position size, margin usage, and profit targets based on parameters such as maximum loss, stop loss, leverage, and risk:reward ratio, with optional entry price support.

Built with a focus on accuracy, usability, and speed, the project reflects a structured, logic-driven approach to real-world trading scenarios.

It is actively used by traders from the "Viver de Cripto" community and demonstrates practical front-end development applied to real use cases.

---

## ⚙️ Features

- 📉 Calculation of **maximum loss**, **position size**, and **margin usage**
- 🎯 **Stop-loss** and **take-profit** calculation based on entry price
- 📈 Support for **Long and Short** operations with configurable leverage
- 🔁 Risk:reward ratio selection from **1:1 up to 1:10**
- 🕓 **Trade history** stored locally
- 📤 Export history to **.xlsx spreadsheet**
- 🌙 **Light/Dark mode** with system detection
- 📄 Built-in **technical documentation**
- 📱 Fully **responsive layout**

## 🚀 How to use

1. Access: https://calculadoraviverdecripto.vercel.app/
2. Enter your **maximum loss**
3. (Optional) Enter the **entry price**
4. Set **direction, stop loss, leverage, and risk:reward**
5. Click **Calculate**
6. View or export results in the **History**
